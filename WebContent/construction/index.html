About Us

Agastiyan Home Innovations Private Limited is an ISO 9001 : 2015 certified Company operating in the verticals of Construction, Interiors, Property Management, Advisories and allied Trading activities.

We believe in delivering quality products and services to all our customers. We understand the hassles faced by customers in reality sector for which we have all the needed solutions.

Our Core values and commitments are being Ethical, Efficient and Environmentally sensitive.

Our strengths are Professional Services, Transparency, Value Preposition and One stop solution.

Innovation, Information Technology and Industry best practices are the driving force for all our organizational and operational processes.

We provide the most inspiring homes with the finest quality materials, value through innovative designs, highest level of craftsmanship and professionalism.

We offer our clients outstanding economics of scale in the construction process by virtue of housing four separate operating divisions under one roof, a rarity in the construction business. The operational model allows us to offer extremely competitive pricing in all of its markets, all the while maintaining superior attention to detail, schedule and budget.

At present, the construction of independent houses is managed by un organized sector. There is no trusted brand who can assure transparency and agreed services to the buyers. Normally the buyer and builder relationship are ruined towards end of the project. In almost all the cases the budget of constructing houses is overshot and buyer tends to borrow over his limit or comprises on items to complete the construction. There is no reconciliation between planned and actual expenses.
